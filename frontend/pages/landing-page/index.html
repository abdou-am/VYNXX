Frequently Asked Questions

Find clear answers to common questions about our services, process, pricing, and how we work to bring your project to life

How much does a project cost?

The cost depends on your project's needs and level of complexity. Each project is different, so we first discuss your goals and requirements, then provide a price that matches exactly what you're looking for

How long does a project take?

The timeline depends on the type and complexity of the project. For example, simple e-commerce video creatives usually take between 24 hours and 3 days, while more advanced projects may take longer depending on the requirements

What do you need to get started?

We start by understanding your business, your goals, and what you want to achieve. A small initial payment is required to begin the project. For web projects, we also schedule a meeting to define everything clearly, then provide a detailed proposal covering scope, timeline, pricing, and technologies

Can I request revisions?

Yes, revisions are included. For video production, up to three revisions are allowed at each stage (script, visuals, voice-over, and editing). For web projects, small adjustments like layout or color changes are included, while new features or major additions may require an additional cost

What services do you offer?

We focus on video production and web development, creating high-quality content and digital experiences tailored to your brand and business needs

Will my project be custom or based on templates?

Every project is fully custom. Websites are built from scratch, and videos are created using professional tools without relying on pre-made templates, ensuring unique and high-quality results
